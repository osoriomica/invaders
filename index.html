Welcome to Urban Invaders!

This is a playful take on the street art installations by French artist Invader and the original space invaders arcade game. You are now a tourist in the search of street art! Use your camera to shoot some tiled invaders!
Hope you enjoy the game!

Stop the invaders before they reach you!
